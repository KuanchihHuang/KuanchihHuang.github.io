About Me

I am currently a research assistant at the Department of Computer Science & Information Engineering, National Taiwan University, under the supervision of Prof. Winston H. Hsu. Prior to that, I worked at ASUS Intelligence Cloud Services (AICS) as an AI software engineer for one year. I received my B.S. degree and M.S. degree in Physics and Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University in 2017 and 2019. My research journey started in the fall of 2020, and my research interests are computer vision and autonomous driving.
