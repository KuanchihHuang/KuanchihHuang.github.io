Kuan-Chih Huang
I am an EECS Ph.D. student (Aug. 2023-) at University of California, Merced, advised by Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang and Dr. Yi-Hsuan Tsai. I was a student researcher at Google DeepMind in 2025. Previously, I was a full-time research assistant, working with Prof. Winston H. Hsu. I received my B.S. in Physics and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University. My current research interests are generative world modeling.
